# Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

This Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") is entered into by and between Consultaion and the Customer, supplementing the Terms of Service.

## 1. Scope and Application
This DPA applies to the processing of personal data (e.g. user account profiles, telemetry data) by Consultaion on behalf of the Customer in the course of providing the Service.

## 2. Roles of the Parties
* **Customer as Controller:** The Customer is the Data Controller of the personal data and prompts submitted to the service.
* **Consultaion as Processor:** Consultaion acts as the Data Processor of such personal data.

## 3. Obligations of the Processor
Consultaion agrees to:
* Process personal data only on documented instructions from the Customer.
* Ensure that persons authorized to process the personal data have committed themselves to confidentiality.
* Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk.
* Assist the Customer in responding to data subject rights requests under GDPR and CCPA.
* Delete or return all personal data to the Customer after the end of the provision of services, in accordance with our retention policy.

## 4. Sub-processors
The Customer authorizes Consultaion to engage sub-processors (including AI model providers and cloud hosting providers) to perform processing activities on personal data. A list of active sub-processors is maintained in our [Model Provider Policy](./model-provider-policy.md).

## 5. Security of Processing
Consultaion implements the following security controls:
* Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
* Automatic PII scrubbing on outbound prompt traffic (if enabled).
* Role-based access control and regular review of log access.
