# Privacy Policy

Consultaion is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our platform.

## 1. Information We Collect
* **Account Credentials:** Email address, display name, password hashes, and subscription details.
* **Debate Telemetry & Inputs:** Prompts, configuration choices, and generated debate outputs.
* **Telemetry & Event logs:** Page views, interactions (e.g., clicks, voting), error events, and technical metadata.
* **Integrations:** API keys you optionally save to your account for direct model access.

## 2. Model Providers & Data Handling
To provide simulations, we send scrubbed prompts to external model providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, DeepSeek, etc.). 
* **PII Scrubbing:** If enabled, our model gateway automatically scrubs common PII (emails, phone numbers) before sending payloads to external APIs.
* **Data Use:** We do not allow third-party providers to train their models on prompts submitted through our unified gateway.

## 3. Data Retention Limits
We apply strict retention policies to minimize the storage of personal and telemetry data:
* **Debates & Runs:** Detailed run content is stored for **365 days** to enable history and sharing, after which it is permanently deleted or fully anonymized.
* **Telemetry & Event Logs:** Analytics logs (PostHog, error tracking) are retained for a maximum of **90 days**.
* **Account & Billing Information:** Retained as long as your account remains active.

## 4. User Rights & Controls
You have full control over your data:
* **Access & Export:** You can view your debate history and export usage statistics via the Dashboard.
* **Data Deletion:** You can delete your account and associated debate history at any time in the Settings page. Deleting your account will queue all personal data for permanent deletion from our active systems.
* **Opt-Out:** You can disable tracking/telemetry in your profile preferences.

## 5. Security
We implement industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest to secure your account data, Saved API keys, and logs.

## 6. Updates & Contact
We may update this policy periodically. If you have questions about our data practices, please contact us at privacy@consultaion.com.
